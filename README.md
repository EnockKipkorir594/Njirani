## What is Njirani 
Njirani is a neighbourhood services platform that connects members of an estate or a 
gated community with trusted local service providers - plumbers, electrician,cleaners,
house helps,painters and more - all within their immediate area.

## The problem it solves 
Right now when a pipe bursts at 9am or a light fitting stops working most kenyans do the 
same thing - they open a whatsapp group and type "Does anyone know a good plumber?."
Then they wait sometimes they get five recommendations, sometimes they get none.
They call numbers that don't that don't pickup, negotiate prices blindly,and have no way 
of knowing if the person showing up at their is reliable or not. 

There is no system, no accountability.No record of who did good work and who didn't.Just a 
word of mouth in a group chat and hope.

Njirani replaces that chaos with a platform is fast, transparent, and trustworthy.

## How it works.
A resident opens Njirani,posts a job - "I need a plumber.Ruaka Estate.blocked drainage." - 
and within minutes,available service providers in that area see the request and place a bid.
The resident reviews the bids,picks the provider whose price and rating they trust, the job 
is confirmed.

When the job is done payment goes through M-PESA directly on the platform - no cash handling no 
awarkward negotiations.The rsident then rates the service provider, and that rating stays permanently 
on their profile.

Every job completed builds a track record.Every rating either raises or lowers a provider's standing on 
the platform.The best providers rise to the top and get more work. The poor ones don't.

## The problems Njirani solves 
**For Residents**
- No more posting on whatsapp and waiing for someone to reply
- No more hiring strangers with zero track record.
- No more cash negotiations at the door.
- Instant access to vetted, rated, nearby service providers.

**For service providers**
- No more depending on a word of mouth or referrals to find work.
- A digital profile that show cases their rating and completed jobs.
- A fair bidding system where skill and reputation win, not connection.
- GUaranteed payment via M-PESA the moment a job is confirmed complete.

## Who uses Njirani 
**Residents(clients)** - people living in estates, apartments, or living in gated communities who need
home services done quickly, reliably and by someone they can trust.They are busy, they value their time
and they want the job done right.

**Service providers(fundis)** - skilled tradespeople and home service workers who want a steady stream of jobs nearby
without relying and refferals and WhatsApp groups.Plumbers, electricians, cleaners, painters,gardeners, security guards
and handymen.

**Estate managers(Admins)** - managers of estates or apartment complexes who want oversight of services being requested
and fulfilled within their property, and who want ensure residents are getting quality service from verified providers.

## The goal 
Njirani exists to make getting a reliable service in your neighbourhood as easy as ordering food online.Fast, fair 
and fully accountable - where quality poviders earn more, residents get better service, and no one has to ask in a WhatsApp 
group ever again.
